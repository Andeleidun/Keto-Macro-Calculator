 For activity levels:
  • A sedentary lifestyle is typical of an office worker who gets no outside exercise.
  • A restaurant employee would have a lightly active lifestyle. 1-3 hours per week of exercise.
  • An avid hiker would have a moderately active lifestyle. 3-5 hours per week of exercise.
  • Manual laborers and those that exercise heavily every day have a highly active lifestyle.
 For goal levels:
  • Make sure that if you set for muscle gain, you are exercising appropriately.
  • If you are after muscle gain, you will have to set a higher protein ratio as well.
  • Set goal level to 1 for maintenance.
 For TDEE:
  • TDEE stands for Total Daily Energy Expenditure.
  • For most people, the calculated TDEE will be quite sufficient.
  • If a medical professional has determined your TDEE, always defer to their value.
 For Protein Ratio:
  • .69-.72 is ideal for maintaing muscle while losing weight.
  • For maintaining weight, use a value from .7-.75.
  • For optimum muscle gain, use a value from .79-.82.
 For Macro %:
  • This is an advanced feature for those that already know their optimum percentages.
  • Carbs shouldn't be set at above 5%, unless your calorie goal is really low.
