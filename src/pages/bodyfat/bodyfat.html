Body Fat % Lean Mass

This body fat estimator uses the US Navy method for estimating rough body fat percentage.
For most adults, the results are accurate to within 1-3%. For quick and easy estimation, it's generally sufficient.
However, there are more accurate methods such as hydrostatic weighing, skin callipers, and bioelectrical impedance.
Men should measure their waist around the navel, and women should measure at the narrowest width.
Measure your neck around the base, below the Adam's Apple for men.
For women, measure your hips around the largest width.
