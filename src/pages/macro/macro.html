How Active Are You?

1 - Sedentary

A sedentary lifestyle is typical of an office worker who gets no outside exercise.

 2 - Lightly Active

A restaurant employee would have a lightly active lifestyle. 1-3 hours per week of exercise.

 3 - Moderately Active

An avid hiker would have a moderately active lifestyle. 3-5 hours per week of exercise.

 4 - Highly Active

Manual laborers and those that exercise heavily every day have a highly active lifestyle.

Please evaluate your lifestyle. Calorie needs will change depending on lifestyle.

What Is Your Goal?

Weight Loss Maintenance Muscle Gain Intensity Easy Moderate Aggressive Intensity Easy Moderate Aggressive

Weight loss and muscle gain will give different ratios for macronutrients.
Make sure that your workouts match your goals selected if you select muscle gain.
If you select aggressive muscle gain and aren't working out sufficiently, you will gain fat as well.
If you select easy and are working out aggressively, you won't have the proper nutrients to take full advantage of your workouts.
Weight loss goal levels are designed for both exercise and not exercising, and aren't as particular.
